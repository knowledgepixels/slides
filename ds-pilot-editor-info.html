## Nanopublishing Pilot at Data Science Info for Editors

This pilot is conducted by Knowledge Pixels, a brand-new startup based in Zurich, Switzerland.

Nanodash is a web tool to browse and publish nanopublications with (relatively) user-friendly web forms.

Connector Page

(open in separate tab)

### Express that you are an editor of Data Science with Nanodash - Follow this link: [https://tinyurl.com/I-am-editor-of-data-science](https://tinyurl.com/I-am-editor-of-data-science) - Login with ORCID - Publish your editor relation as nanopublication - Follow instructions to publish your own introduction (connecting your signature keys to your ORCID identifier)
### Workflow 1. Authors follow link to connector page 2. Authors publish nanopublications 3. Authors link nanopublication identifier(s) in submission form 4. Editor is assigned, reviewers are invited, etc. 5. Reviewers are encouraged to also check the nanopublications, and report about them in the review form 6. ...
### Workflow (continued) 6. Editor checks manuscript, reviews, and nanopublications, with help from Knowledge Pixels if needed, and makes a decision 7. If accepted, the final acceptance is also published as a nanopublication (by editor or Knowledge Pixels) 8. Nanopublications are formally published together with article (might get their own DOI later on)
